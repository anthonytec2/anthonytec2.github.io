Hi I am Anthony Bisulco and I am a 2nd year Electrical and Computer Engineer at Northeastern University. This website is devoted to projects I have worked on, in the past and currently am developing. As so far I have no specific focus in electrical engineering although I really enjoy Digital Signal Processing(DSP) specifically Compressive Sensing(CS), digital design including FPGA development, optimization methods, machine learning and radar systems. I actively pursue each of these concentrations in my research at Northeastern University in millimeter wave body scanning. Along with research throughout the semester, I participate in multiple hackathons which you will see some of my team’s work in the projects section.
