Robot Arm V1

Robot Arm V1

I did not do anything of the building of this it was really a group project I didn't do much of the work I had collaborated with a few other people with writing the code. The robotic arm is made with with light weight see through plastic, a lazy susan joint, wood, pneumatics set, 4 servos and a robotic claw. The Arduino's job in all of this was to control the servos from the 2 potentiometers mounted on a bread board. The code allows the servos to move faster as the value on the potentiometer increases allowing fast time for moving the claw. The goal of this overall project was to move the specified items you see in the taped square to the goal boxes which are half cut milk jugs. Each item is a different point value there are pvc pipes, batteries, iron nails and pencils. I also controlled the robot arm bellow made by another kid with an amazing building ability this is controlled by and rc controller which has a rc receiver hooked up to the servos on this robot. During the competition the robot actually failed out with the claw because its servo connection was loose so we just knocked everything above the line and got points.
