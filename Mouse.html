Morse Trap

This project was developed for my high school Science Olympiad Mouse Trap Vehicle. Which basically had an egg attached to the front and would stop after a known distance. This was one of my first fully mechanical projects which was really fun to work on. The wheels were attached to a threaded axel which had two wing nuts on each axel. As the car begins to move the wing nuts move down the axel to the end of the wheel which causes them to lock up the wheels and stop the car.
