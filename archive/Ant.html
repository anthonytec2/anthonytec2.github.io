Loop Antenna

Loop Antenna

The above was a loop antenna that I built based off the idea of my VLF project seen previously in the first section. This antenna features cross beams that are about a meter and a half on each cross frame. Then there are approximately 50 turns of copper wire around the cross beams. I developed this whole antenna from cutting the wood to turning the copper wire around the coil. Initially, I did test it on my VLF project and it worked pretty well. Although, I actually enjoy it to work on the HF/VHF/UHF bands for listening. Since, the antenna is so large it is able to receive signals very well in the directions which the EM field is orthogonal to the loop.
