Hi, my name is Anthony Bisulco and I am a machine learning research engineer at Samsung Research America in the Samsung Artificial Intelligence Center(SAIC) NY working on robotics. Previously, I was a graduate student in Electrical and Computer Engineer at Cornell Tech(NYC). This website is devoted to my various personal projects and blog. My areas of interest are in signal processing specifically compressive sensing(CS), machine learning, biomedical imaging, optimization methods, parallel programming, computational accelerators and radar systems.
